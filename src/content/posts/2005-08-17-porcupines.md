A conversation I overheard yesterday from the next room: **Hannah:** Mummy? Do you know what porcupines are? **Nicola:** Yes. **Hannah:** What are they then? **Nicola:** Well, they're animals with lots of spikes, a bit like hedgehogs. _\[Confused pause.\]_ **Hannah:** So why do people say "Don't tell porcu-pines"? **Nicola:** No, sweetheart, that's _porky pies_. It means lies: don't tell lies. **Hannah _\[penny dropping\]_:** Ohhh! Aside: Friends and family will know that porcupines (or one porcupine in particular) will have a special significance for Hannah one day. She just doesn't know that yet. :-)
