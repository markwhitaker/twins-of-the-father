A conversation I overheard yesterday from the next room:

**Hannah:** Mummy? Do you know what porcupines are?<br/>
**Nicola:** Yes.<br/>
**Hannah:** What are they then?<br/>
**Nicola:** Well, they're animals with lots of spikes, a bit like hedgehogs.<br/>
_\[Confused pause.\]_<br/>
**Hannah:** So why do people say "Don't tell porcu-pines"?<br/>
**Nicola:** No, sweetheart, that's _porky pies_. It means lies: don't tell lies.<br/>
**Hannah _\[penny dropping\]_:** Ohhh!

Aside: Friends and family will know that porcupines (or one porcupine in particular) will have a special significance for Hannah one day. She just doesn't know that yet. :-)
