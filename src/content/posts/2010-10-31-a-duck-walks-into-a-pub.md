Seriously, at what age do children start making up better jokes? Or even just jokes that are mildly funny? Here we continue to wait for that day in vain. Today's effort from Lauren:

> This duck goes into a pub, right, and he says, "Can I have a cucumber, please?" and the man in the bar says "No, we only do beer, wine and orange juice," so the duck says, "Right, can I have orange juice please?" and the man says, "OK, I'll pour you a glass."

Needless to say, the girls found it hilarious.
