Getting dressed this morning, the girls decided to put me on the spot with some rapid-fire arithmetic.

**Lauren:** Daddy, what makes three and three?<br/>
**Me _(blearily)_:** What...?<br/>
**Hannah:** Six!<br/>
_\[Much chuckling and cackling ensues because they beat me to it and I am a dunce.\]_<br/>
**Hannah:** Daddy, what makes... four... and four?<br/>
**Lauren:** Eight!<br/>
_\[Shrieks of laughter at expense of village idiot.\]_<br/>
**Lauren:** What makes... nine and nine?!

_\[Pause.\]_

**Hannah:** Daddy, what makes nine and nine?<br/>
**Me:** Eighteen.<br/>
**Hannah:** Oh.<br/>
_\[They chuckle for reasons best know to themselves.\]_<br/>
**Hannah:** What makes... ten and ten?<br/>
**Lauren:** Infinity?<br/>
**Me:** No, twenty.<br/>
**Lauren:** So, what makes a hundred and a hundred?<br/>
**Hannah:** Infinity?<br/>
**Me:** No, two hundred.<br/>
**Lauren:** Is two hundred and two hundred inifinity?

And so on. I can see this taking some time.
