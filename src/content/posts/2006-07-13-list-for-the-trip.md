![](../../images/2006-07-13-list-for-the-trip.jpg)

1.  Lunch
2.  bag
3.  thining caps _(thinking caps)_
4.  listening ears
5.  good manners
6.  treyners _(trainers)_

8.  Bottle _(i.e. of water)_
9.  hat _(it means sun-hat, although one of the girls tried to take a woolly bobble hat)_
10.  Biran _(brain)_
11.  sunglasses
12.  books
13.  suncrim _(sun cream)_
