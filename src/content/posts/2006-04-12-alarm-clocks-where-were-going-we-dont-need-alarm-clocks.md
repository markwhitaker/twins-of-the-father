There was a raging debate going on in our bed at 7 o'clock this morning. It went something like this: **Hannah:** I'm Darth Vader! RAAAARRGGHHH!!! _(Note: this is a Darth Vader who wears a nightie and roars like a tiger. No heavy breathing required.)_ _Parents "shriek" with "fright"._ **Lauren:** I'm Barbie! _Parents sigh with relief._ **Hannah:** I'm Darth Vader!! **Lauren:** No-o! _I'm_ Darth Vader now! **Hannah:** OK. **Lauren:** I'm Darth Vader!! RAARGGHHHHH!!! _Shriek!_ **Hannah:** I'm Darth Vader!! RAA... **Lauren:** No! You're Barbie! **Hannah:** But I want to be Darth Vader! _Simultaneously:_ **Lauren:** Mummy, Hannah won't be Barbie! **Hannah:** Daddy, it's _my_ turn to be Darth Vader! And so on, forever. It's funny how quickly we can be out of bed some days.
