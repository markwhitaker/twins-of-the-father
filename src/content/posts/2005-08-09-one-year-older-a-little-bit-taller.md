Hannah and Lauren are five today! Lauren informed us this morning that her nightie, which used to come all the way down to _here_, this morning only reaches to _here_. "But the nightie is still the same size... so I must have grown because I'm five!" Hannah was able to confirm that the same phenomenon applies to her pyjamas. And you can't get much more scientific than that.
