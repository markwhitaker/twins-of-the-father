![Funny face](https://twins.bitrot.net/wp-content/uploads/2007/01/funnyface.jpg)A conversation with one of the girls yesterday shortly after taking the attached photo with my cameraphone.

"I wish I had a phone like that."<br/>
"You don't need a phone, sweetheart, they're only for grown-ups."<br/>
"Where did you get it from?"<br/>
"From a shop."<br/>
"Did they have another one exactly the same?"<br/>
"Yes, I think so."<br/>
"Well when I'm a mummy can you take me to that shop and show me so I can buy that one?"<br/>
"What a terrific idea. Of course I can."

It's lovely to think I'll still be needed. An extra bonus point to the first reader who can correctly identify the twin in question... :-)
