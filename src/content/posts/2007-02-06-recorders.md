The girls got recorders for Christmas. They're both downstairs abusing them now. So far they have only learnt one note: that piercing one you get when you blow too hard with all holes uncovered. Shriek! Shriek! Shriek-shriek-shriek! Mike Oldfield this ain't. I am approaching the point of despair. A word from the wise: if your children ever come into posession of recorders, buy ear muffs or leave home.
