December fever is upon us. It barely seems a couple of months since last Christmas but as I type, the girls are dancing round the landing shrieking a song they just made up that appears to be called "It's Four More Days Till Christmas." Today is the 3rd of December. They're supposed to be going to bed. The next 22 days are what makes or breaks a man. Wish me luck.
