I can't believe it's been so long since [I last shared the girls' jokes with you](/two-jokes/) (over a year now!). Time to make amends with an absolute belter from Lauren this morning.

**Lauren:** Daddy, do you want to hear a really bad joke?<br/>
**Me:** OK.<br/>
**Lauren:** OK, so... what's the difference about a _witch_...<br/>
**Me:** Between, Lauren. What's the difference _between_.<br/>
**Lauren:** Oh yeah, OK, right... what's the difference between a _witch_ and...<br/>
**Hannah:** A biscuit!<br/>
**Lauren:** Hannah!<br/>
**Me:** Hannah, don't interrupt, let Lauren tell her joke. Go on Lauren.<br/>
**Lauren:** OK, so, right, what's the difference about a _witch_...<br/>
**Me:** Between.<br/>
**Hannah:** Stop interrupting Lauren, Daddy.<br/>
**Me:** Shush, Hannah.<br/>
**Lauren:** ..._between_ a _witch_ and... erm...<br/>
**Hannah:** A biscuit!<br/>
**Lauren/Me: HANNAH!!**<br/>
**Lauren:** Hang on, it's in my joke book... _\[fetches joke book\]_... Oh yeah, right: what's the difference between a wizard and the letters K, A, S, M and E?<br/>
**Me:** I don't know.<br/>
**Lauren:** One makes spells and the other spells MAKES!

It's all in the timing.
