Wow, how time flies. The girls are now 10 but you'll be glad to hear they're just as much fun (and just as inept at performing basic personal hygiene routines independently) as they've always been. Their latest thing is the word "slash", used to combine multiple concepts into one exciting new hybrid. We've just come back from a weekend away, where at 10:30pm on Saturday the unmistakeable sounds of monkeying about were still drifting down from the girls' bedroom. I went up to investigate. Both girls were on the floor between their beds, along with all their bedclothes, helpless with laughter. The light was on. They both attempted that face that says, "Gosh, how did this happen? I could swear I was just asleep." Spotting an opportunity to blame Hannah for the noise, Lauren clutched her face. "Hannah kicked me in the gums-slash-chin-slash-cheek-slash-jaw!" she cried. "Yeah," muttered Hannah, "and she started moaning-slash-wailing."
