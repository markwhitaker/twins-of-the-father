From Lauren at tea-time tonight:

> Imagine if you had two rabbits, yeah, and they were both called Penny, and you were dropping one of them off at the train station, right, and then her owner said, "Let's go and pick up Penny," but you thought she meant Penny the _pasta!!_

It turns out she was referring to [penne](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Penne). Hannah laughed so hard she almost choked.
